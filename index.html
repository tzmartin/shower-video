Video and Gif management plugin for Shower

Robert Haritonov, @operatino

Insert video to your slide

<section class="slide"><div>
    <video>
        <source src="videos/spec.mp4" type="video/mp4;">
    <video>
</div></section>

Cover mode

Add slide cover videos exactly as with images in Shower:

<section class="slide cover [h, w, h w]"><div>
    <video>
        <source src="videos/spec.mp4" type="video/mp4;">
    <video>
</div></section>

Cover mode

By default, video are higher than slide content. to put text over the video, add .background class:

<video class="background">
    <source src="videos/spec.mp4" type="video/mp4;">
<video>
